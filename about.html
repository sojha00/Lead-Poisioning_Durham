About This Project

This website is dedicated to raising awareness about lead contamination in Durham parks. Our goal is to inform the public, provide resources, and encourage community action to address this issue.

Why It Matters

Lead exposure can have severe health consequences, especially for children. By spreading awareness and advocating for solutions, we can help create safer environments for everyone.
