Ways You Can Make a Difference

Lead contamination is a serious issue, but there are ways you can help protect your community.

1. Raise Awareness

Share information about lead poisoning in Durham parks with your neighbors and on social media.

2. Contact Local Officials

Encourage city leaders to take action by funding clean-up efforts and testing more areas for contamination.

3. Volunteer

Join local environmental groups that work on testing, advocacy, and community education.

4. Donate

Support organizations dedicated to environmental justice and public health.
